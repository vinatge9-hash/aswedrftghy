About Niyantri Coffee

Our journey is rooted in small-batch roasting, seasonal beans, and a warm, rustic atmosphere that invites you to slow down and savor.

Our Story

Niyantri Coffee began as a tiny kettle-and-kiln project in Hyderabad, blending traditional Indian coffee heritage with modern, artisanal roasting techniques. We source ethically, roast in small batches, and invite our community to taste the progress with every cup.

Our rustic space is designed to feel like a friend’s cozy kitchen—wood tones, soft lighting, and the aroma of freshly ground beans in the air.

Roast Philosophy

Slow roasting to enhance depth and sweetness, highlighting origin notes.

Community First

Tickets for barista trainings and cupping sessions fund local initiatives.

Sustainable Sourcing

We work with farms that practice shade-grown, fair-trade methods whenever possible.
